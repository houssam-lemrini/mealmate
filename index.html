Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MealMate

How do I track my meals and calories?

MealMate makes tracking simple! Just search for any food in our extensive database, select the portion size, and add it to your daily log. Our smart scanner also lets you quickly add packaged foods by scanning their barcode.

Can I customize my nutrition goals?

Absolutely! MealMate allows you to set personalized daily calorie, macronutrient, and micronutrient goals based on your fitness objectives. Whether you're looking to lose weight, build muscle, or maintain, we've got you covered.

Is my data secure and private?

Your privacy is our top priority. All your personal data and nutrition information are encrypted and stored securely. We never share your information with third parties without your explicit consent.

Does MealMate work with fitness trackers?

Yes! MealMate integrates seamlessly with popular fitness trackers like Apple Watch, Fitbit, Garmin, and others. Sync your workout data to get a complete picture of your daily calorie balance.

Can I create and save my own recipes?

Definitely! Our recipe builder lets you create custom recipes with multiple ingredients. Once saved, you can quickly log your entire meal with just one click, making meal prep tracking effortless.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes! MealMate is available on both iOS and Android. Download our app from the App Store or Google Play to track your meals on the go with all the features of our web platform.
